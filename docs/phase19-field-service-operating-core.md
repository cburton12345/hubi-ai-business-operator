# Phase 19 Field Service Operating Core

Phase 19 expands the platform from AI marketing and CRM into an all-in-one business operator for local service companies.

## Delivered

- Customer records with contact, location, notes, source lead linkage, and AI summary placeholder.
- Estimate foundation with line items, totals, internal notes, and manual follow-up drafts.
- Job foundation with schedule window, service area, assigned user placeholder, dispatcher notes, and AI next action.
- Invoice/payment placeholder with line items, totals, due status, manual payment notes, and no payment processor connection.
- `/app/service` command center for customers, estimates, jobs, invoices, pipeline value, unscheduled work, and unpaid invoices.
- Navigation from the main app shell and workspace dashboard.

## Safety And Scope

- No automatic messages are sent.
- No Stripe, payment processor, calendar, route planning, or technician app integrations are connected.
- Estimates and invoices are internal/manual records until a later publishing and payment phase.
- The architecture prepares clean tables for future customer portal, dispatch, routing, recurring service, and inventory modules.
