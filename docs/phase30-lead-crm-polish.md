# Phase 30: Lead CRM Polish

Phase 30 confirms and documents the lead-management foundation already present in the app.

## Completed CRM capabilities

- Lead search by name, email, phone, brand, or lead type.
- Brand, status, qualification, and priority filters.
- CSV export route.
- Lead scoring foundation with grades and reasons.
- Lead assignment to workspace users.
- Lead event timeline on detail pages.
- Duplicate detection hints using email or phone.
- Lead-to-service customer conversion.

## Product intent

These pieces make the CRM usable for external businesses instead of only internal admin review. Leads can be triaged, assigned, searched, exported, scored, and converted into service operations.

## Safety

- Duplicate detection is advisory only.
- Assignments do not notify users automatically.
- Reply drafts and follow-ups remain manual.
