# Phase 40: Local Service Intake

Phase 40 confirms the local service intake flows already present in the app.

## Completed local service pieces

- Public forms default local service brands to quote requests.
- Public forms support appointment and quote lead types.
- Service interest, location/service area, appointment window, and urgency are stored in `local_service_lead_details`.
- Leads can be converted into service customers, estimate drafts, and unscheduled jobs.

## Product intent

Local service businesses can capture service-area leads, quote requests, and appointment requests, then move them into the operating workflow.

## Safety

- Appointment requests do not auto-book a calendar.
- Quote requests do not create final pricing automatically.
- Customer messages remain manual.
