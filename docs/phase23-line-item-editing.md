# Phase 23 Line Item Editing

Phase 23 makes estimates and invoices financially usable.

## Delivered

- Estimate line items can be added, edited, and removed.
- Invoice line items can be added, edited, and removed.
- Estimate and invoice subtotal/total values recalculate after every line item change.
- Detail pages show editable name, description, quantity, unit price, and calculated total.

## Safety

- Estimate and invoice edits remain internal records.
- No estimate is sent automatically.
- No invoice is charged or sent to a payment processor.
