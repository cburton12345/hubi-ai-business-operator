# Phase 37: New Lead Notifications

Phase 37 adds in-app new lead notifications through the existing operator alert system.

## What changed

- Alert scan now creates a `new-leads-today` alert when new leads arrive in the last 24 hours.
- Severity rises when five or more new leads need review.
- The alert links to the filtered lead dashboard.
- Roadmap marks new lead notifications complete.

## Product intent

External businesses need a clear place to see that fresh leads are waiting without the platform sending email or SMS automatically.

## Safety

- Notifications are in-app only.
- No customer or operator messages are sent automatically.
- Assignment and follow-up remain manual.
