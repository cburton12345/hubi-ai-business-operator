# Phase 17 Business Intake Polish

Phase 17 improves external customer lead capture so each business model collects the fields needed for useful CRM follow-up and AI lead intelligence.

## Delivered

- Local service forms continue to support quote and appointment requests with service interest, location, appointment window, and urgency.
- Rental forms now collect rental item type, start date, end date, delivery need, and location.
- Software forms now collect company, role, current system, units/accounts managed, and demo request intent.
- Marketplace forms now support buyer, seller, bidder, and consignor lead types, with asset category and estimated value fields.
- Legal-sensitive intake now collects case type, incident date, state/location, injury type, attorney status, treatment status, consent, and disclaimer acknowledgement.
- Public lead schema and database lead type constraint now accept bidder and consignor without enabling any external routing.

## Safety

- No messages are sent automatically.
- No leads are routed externally.
- Legal-sensitive submissions still require consent and disclaimer acknowledgement.
- All detail data remains workspace-scoped and stored with the lead for manual review.
