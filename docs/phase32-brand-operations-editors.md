# Phase 32: Brand Operations Editors

Phase 32 confirms and documents the brand setup editors already present in the app.

## Completed brand setup pieces

- Services editor.
- Service areas editor.
- Offers editor.
- SEO keywords editor.
- Landing page planner.
- Brand identity, phone, email, logo, domain, tone, risk, and approval settings.

## Product intent

The AI operator needs real brand inputs before it can produce useful work. These editors are the main place external businesses define what they sell, where they serve customers, what offers are real, and which keywords or landing pages matter.

## Safety

- Editors store setup data only.
- They do not publish pages.
- They do not launch ads.
- They do not send offers automatically.
