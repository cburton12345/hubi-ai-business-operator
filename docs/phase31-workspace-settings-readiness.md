# Phase 31: Workspace Settings Readiness

Phase 31 confirms and documents the workspace settings and external onboarding readiness already present in the app.

## Completed SaaS readiness pieces

- Organization profile settings.
- Timezone and report email.
- Manual export policy.
- Plan and billing placeholders without Stripe.
- Usage tracking placeholder.
- External customer onboarding checklist.
- Client onboarding flow entry points.

## Product intent

External businesses need a place to configure their organization before the platform becomes their day-to-day operating system. This phase keeps billing and integrations as placeholders until the core workflows are stable.

## Safety

- Stripe is not connected.
- Usage data is a placeholder.
- Settings are workspace-scoped and require manage permissions.
