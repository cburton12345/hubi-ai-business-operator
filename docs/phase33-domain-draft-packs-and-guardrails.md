# Phase 33: Domain Draft Packs And Guardrails

Phase 33 confirms the domain-specific AI draft and safety coverage already present in the operator.

## Completed draft packs

- Local service city/service page recommendations.
- Google Business Profile post drafts.
- Review request drafts.
- Follow-up email and text drafts.
- Rental SEO recommendations.
- Software demo nurture and comparison page ideas.
- Marketplace buyer/seller campaign ideas.
- Auction content draft workflows.

## Completed guardrails

- No legal advice for legal-sensitive brands.
- No compensation or outcome guarantees.
- Attorney relationship disclaimer handling in legal intake.
- No fake pricing, reviews, licensing, insurance, testimonials, or guarantees.
- Brand facts only for generated content.

## What remains intentionally unchecked

- External publishing.
- Google Business Profile API.
- Facebook API.
- Google Ads API.
- SMS/email sending.
- Any automatic public response or legal-sensitive routing.
