# Phase 28: Technician Workflow

Phase 28 adds a mobile-friendly technician workflow page.

## What changed

- Added `/app/service/tech`.
- Shows scheduled and in-progress jobs due today or tomorrow.
- Lets operators update job status, dispatcher notes, completion notes, and next action.
- Keeps route planning and service command center linked together.

## Product intent

This gives field teams a practical work queue without requiring a native app, GPS tracking, calendar sync, or customer messaging integration.

## Safety

- No customer messages are sent.
- No payments are collected.
- Schedule dates are not changed from the technician workflow.
- Completion remains a manual operator action.
