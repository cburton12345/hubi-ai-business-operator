# Phase 54 SEO Autopilot Foundation

Phase 54 adds a Soro-style SEO autopilot foundation without connecting live ranking, analytics, CMS, or publishing APIs.

## Completed

- Added `/app/seo` as a focused SEO Autopilot page.
- Generates draft-only topic clusters from each brand's services, service areas, SEO keywords, landing pages, CTA, target customer, and tone.
- Creates SEO blog drafts, service page drafts, city page drafts, metadata suggestions, and internal-link suggestions in existing `ai_drafts`.
- Adds SEO calendar items for review without publishing anything.
- Adds SEO recommendations that explain what should be reviewed before keys unlock live data.
- Keeps Search Console, Analytics, CMS publishing, and external automation key-gated.

## Safety

- No external SEO, CMS, Google, or publishing APIs are connected.
- Generated content is draft-only and review-first.
- Drafts preserve existing guardrails against fake guarantees, fake pricing, invented reviews, unverified licensing/insurance, legal claims, and misleading results.

## Future Key-Gated Additions

- Search Console query and ranking ingestion.
- Analytics traffic and conversion feedback.
- CMS publishing after approval.
- Internal link graph from crawled/published URLs.
- Daily autopilot recommendations with explicit workspace controls.
